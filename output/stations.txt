Denali Ridge Fire Station is located in Healy, Denali Borough. Established in 1984, it has a staffing level of 28 and maintains six pieces of apparatus. The station covers an area of 320 square miles and responds to approximately 1,450 calls annually. Its primary hazards include wildland fires, remote cabin emergencies, and highway accidents.

Glacier Bay Fire Station operates in Gustavus, Gustavus Area. It was established in 1999 with 18 staff members and four pieces of apparatus. Covering an area of 210 square miles, the station handles around 620 calls per year. Its main hazards involve wildland fires, marine rescues, and remote lodge incidents.

Kenai Fjord Fire Station is situated in Seward, Kenai Peninsula. Founded in 1975, it employs 36 personnel and has seven pieces of apparatus. The station covers 180 square miles and reports about 2,100 calls annually. Its primary hazards include port incidents, wildland fires, and avalanche responses.
